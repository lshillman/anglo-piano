Hi, I’m Luke.

I made Anglo Piano to help myself transcribe things and puzzle out chords without excessive hunting-and-pecking. It works pretty well for me as someone who is mostly illiterate and plays by ear. Your mileage may vary.

If you find this tool helpful, I’d love to know! Contact me via concertina.net, mastodon, youtube, or email to ‘hi’ at this domain.

If you feel like it, you can buy me a coffee.
